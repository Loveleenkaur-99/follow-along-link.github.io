The irresistible aroma of Indianspices greets you as you enter Namaste India, a hidden gem centrally located in the heart of Chinatown. One of the nicest Indian restaurants in Canada, the spacious and well-decorated place is sure to win your heart with the delicious food and warm hospitality. All dishes are spot on, prepared with fresh ingredients and spices to give you a taste of rich Indian flavours. The lovely atmosphere and impeccable servicea are winners all the way, along with a superb range of vegetarian, vegan and non-vegetarian options to suit all palates. Their sumptuous lunch and dinner buffet is highly recommended.
